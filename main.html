Summary

*Inception* is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's dreams. Cobb is offered a chance to erase his criminal record by performing the impossible: planting an idea into someone's subconscious, a process known as "inception." However, as the mission unfolds, the line between dreams and reality begins to blur.
